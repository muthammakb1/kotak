Open a Savings Account Online - Done in Minutes

Opening a new Kotak Savings Account is easy. Here’s what you need to do to kickstart your savings journey with Kotak Bank.

Online Account Opening (Instant - No Branch Visit Required)

Step 1: Apply Online

Visit Kotak Bank's website or download the Kotak Mobile Banking App. Click 'Open a Savings Account' and enter your basic details: name (as per PAN), PAN number, Aadhaar number, and Aadhaar-registered mobile number.

Step 2: Verify Your Identity (OTP / Video KYC)

An OTP will be sent to your Aadhaar-linked mobile number. Verify it to proceed. Complete a short Video KYC from your phone - no branch visit, no physical paperwork. Ensure a stable internet connection and keep a blank white paper and pen handy for signature upload.

Step 3: Your Account is Ready

Once Video KYC is approved, your Savings Account is activated. Your Virtual Debit Card is available instantly on the app; a physical Debit Card is dispatched to your registered address.

Would you prefer to visit a branch? Walk into any Kotak Mahindra Bank branch with your PAN card, Aadhaar card, and a passport-size photograph. Our executives will guide you through the process.

Eligibility Criteria for Savings Account Opening

Want a savings account in India that gives you diverse benefits beyond just savings? Kotak Bank’s Savings Account checks this box easily. With the instant account opening facility, it’s now easier than ever to start saving up for your future. Before you open an instant bank account, it’s crucial to be aware of the eligibility criteria. The criteria may vary based on the type of account you choose.

Broadly, however, you need to meet the following conditions to open a Savings Account with Kotak Bank.

  • Be a resident Indian
  • Be at least 18 years of age

Note: Foreign nationals residing in India, members of Hindu Undivided Families (HUFs), HUFs themselves and minor children can also open certain types of Kotak Savings Accounts.

How to Open a New Bank Account

Opening a new Kotak Savings Account is easy. Here’s what you need to do to kickstart your savings journey with Kotak Bank.

Step 1: Visit your nearest Kotak Mahindra Bank Branch

The on-ground executives would be ready to assist you with the process


Step 2: Submit the following documents

  1. Identity Proof - Driving License, Passport, Voter's ID, Aadhaar Card etc.
  2. Address Proof - Passport, Driving License, Voter's ID, Aadhaar Card, Utility Bill, Bank Statement, Bank Account Passbook (Updated and no more than 3 months old)
  3. Signature Proof - PAN Card, Driving License, Passport
  4. Latest Passport Size Photographs


Step 3: Complete the biometric process

Complete the KYC process to activate your Kotak savings account.

Note: Kindly ensure you have a strong internet connection for due completion of video KYC. Don’t forget to enable live location access for the same. Additionally, keep a plain white paper and a blue/black pen ready to upload your signature during this step.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Savings Account?

A Savings Account is a deposit account that lets you safely keep your money with a bank while earning interest on your balance. You can deposit and withdraw funds as per your needs, making it ideal for everyday banking, building an emergency fund, and saving towards your goals. With Kotak, you can open a Savings Account online instantly using Aadhaar-based Video KYC - no branch visit required.

Types of Savings Accounts at Kotak Bank

Zero Balance Account (811): A fully digital savings account with no minimum balance requirement.

Everyday+ Savings Account: Designed for daily spending with cashback and lifestyle benefits.

Silk Savings Programme: Curated for women with exclusive rewards and offers.

Junior Savings Account: Helps children learn to save with a personalised debit card.

Civil Pension Account: For pensioners, with NMC waivers and Grand Savings benefits.

Key Features and Benefits of a Kotak Savings Account

  • Earn FD-like interest with the ActivMoney auto-sweep feature
  • Instant online account opening with Video KYC
  • Zero balance option with Kotak 811
  • 24x7 digital banking via mobile and net banking
  • Everyday offers on shopping, dining, and travel
  • Health insurance and lifestyle benefits on select variants

Eligibility and Documents Required to Open a Savings Account

To open a Kotak Savings Account, you need to:

  • Be a resident Indian
  • Be at least 18 years of age

Documents required: PAN card, Aadhaar card (Identity & Address proof), and a recent passport-size photograph. Foreign nationals residing in India, HUFs, and minor children can also open certain account types.

Savings Account Interest Rates

Kotak Savings Accounts earn interest of 2.50% p.a. across all account balances (effective July 9th, 2025), applicable to both Resident and Non-resident (NRE/NRO) Accounts. With the ActivMoney feature, balances above your set threshold sweep into a Fixed Deposit, letting you earn higher FD-like interest while keeping your funds fully accessible.

How to Open a Savings Account Online

Opening a Kotak Savings Account is quick and paperless:

  • Visit Kotak Bank’s website or download the Kotak Mobile Banking App
  • Click ‘Open a Savings Account’ and enter your basic details
  • Verify the OTP sent to your Aadhaar-linked mobile number
  • Complete a short Video KYC from your phone
  • Your account is activated and a Virtual Debit Card is available instantly
