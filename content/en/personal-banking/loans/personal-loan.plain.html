Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Personal Loan?

A Personal Loan is an unsecured loan you can use for almost any purpose - weddings, travel, medical needs, or home renovation - without pledging any collateral. You repay it in fixed monthly instalments over a chosen tenure.

What is the maximum amount I can borrow?

With a Kotak Personal Loan you can borrow up to ₹1 crore, subject to your income, credit profile, and eligibility.

What is the interest rate on a Kotak Personal Loan?

Interest rates start at 10.99%* per annum. The exact rate depends on your credit score, income, and loan tenure.

What are the documents required to apply?

You typically need your PAN card, Aadhaar card, income proof (salary slips/bank statements), and a recent photograph. Most steps are completed digitally via Video KYC.

How long does approval and disbursal take?

Eligible applicants can get instant in-principle approval, with the loan amount disbursed directly to the bank account - often within a few hours of completing KYC.

Can I prepay or foreclose my Personal Loan?

Yes, Kotak allows part-prepayment and foreclosure of Personal Loans as per the applicable terms and charges disclosed at the time of sanction.
